Our Vision: Gacha Meets Growtopia

Launched on February 19, 2024, Firefly Private Server was born from a simple dream: to blend the addictive gacha mechanics of games like Honkai: Star Rail, Genshin Impact, and Wuthering Waves with the creative sandbox freedom of Growtopia.

Our mission is bold—we're building the first true gacha experience within Growtopia. Imagine pulling for limited 5-star items with unique animations, managing your primogems (or our equivalent!), strategizing around pity systems, and collecting exclusive characters—all while building, trading, and socializing in the world you love.

For nearly a year, our passionate team has poured countless hours into crafting custom items, implementing wish banner systems, hosting engaging events, and listening to community feedback. We're not just running a server—we're cultivating an experience where every player can feel the rush of a legendary pull and the satisfaction of completing their collection.

"We believe Growtopia's potential goes far beyond what's been explored. Firefly is our answer to that potential— a server where innovation meets nostalgia, where gacha excitement meets sandbox creativity."
